The Two Towers

{{ current_datetime }}

This is the second movie in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. It was released in 2002.

Synopsis

The Fellowship of the Ring is scattered. Frodo and Sam meet an unwelcome creature; Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli join forces with the horse lords of Rohan to defend Middle Earth against the growing army of Saruman. Merry and Pippin, previously captured by Uruk-hai, encounter the Ents, ancient tree shepherds of Fangorn Forest.

Key Events (Spoiler Alert)

  • Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli continue pursuing the rescue of Merry and Pippin and encounter exiled riders of Rohan who had killed the Orcs that had captured Merry and Pippin
  • Frodo and Sam are ambushed in their sleep by Gollum but were able to capture him. He agrees to lead them to Mordor
  • Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli follow the trail of the other two hobbits into Fangorn forest where they encounter a resurrected Gandalf who is now Gandalf the White
  • The four go to Rohan to help King Theodin who was possessed by Saruman. Merry and Pippin meet Treebeard, an Ent (tree shepherd) who is a friend of Gandalf.
  • To avoid Saruman's army, Aragorn and the others take shelter in Helm's Deep. They aid the people of Rohan and elves of Lothlorien in fending off a massive seige on Helm's Deep
  • Frodo and Sam are captured by Faramir, Boromir's younger brother who is tempted by the One Ring.
  • Faramir, who is tempted to take the ring to please his father, decides to let Frodo and Sam free. Meanwhile, the Ents march a siege on Isengard against Saruman for his destruction of the forest of Fangorn
